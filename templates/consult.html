Consultation

The goal of an Ayurvedic consultation is to determine the individual's Ayurvedic Constitution (Prakriti) and imbalances (Vikriti). Based on this assessment, personalized healthcare plans are created, focusing on prevention and individualized lifestyle medicine.

Ayurvedic consultation plays a crucial role in Ayurvedic medicine. It involves a comprehensive assessment of an individual's health history, physical, psychological, physiological, and genetic makeup to create personalized healthcare plans. While Ayurveda is not licensed as a medical discipline in the United States, it can be used as a complementary therapy in combination with standard, conventional medical care. It's important to consult with a healthcare provider before starting any Ayurvedic treatments or therapies, especially in specific situations like pregnancy or nursing.
